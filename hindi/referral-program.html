JDY Referral Program

Be a changemaker influencer - JDY Referral Program

Jagriti Yatra aims to attract the best candidates and for that, no one can be better than the current on-boarded candidates who can make JDY reach and create value in the lives of people from every part of INDIA! After you are selected and onboarded, tell your friends, family, and known about JDY, and you will be paid INR 200 per on-boarded candidate you refer.

Note

  • After your referred candidate registers, fill out the Google form and share the details with us.
  • Maximum cashback will be 50% of the Fees paid by the referring candidate.
  • The cashback will be credited to the account from which the payment was made.
  • It is important that the Yatri ID, email address, and phone number of the referring candidate and the referred candidate match the JDY system details.
  • You can share about JDY verbally, via whatsapp, email or from social media platforms.
  • Do not fill up the form multiple times for the same referred candidate.
  • The final amount of the cashback will be credited deducting 2.5% as a processing fee.
  • The onboarded candidate is the one who is selected and has paid the Yatra fees.
  • It is not applicable for 100% sponsored candidates.
  • Google Form will be active in the JDY account, once you are an on-boarded candidate
